Our Services

Technology that fits your business, not the other way around.

Every engagement starts with understanding your problem. We work alongside you to scope, build, and refine — with projects starting under $500 CAD.

01

AI Strategy & Integration

AI works best when it's built for your specific problem. We help you identify where it fits, prototype solutions together, and refine them until they genuinely save you time and money.

Discovery & Assessment

We start by understanding your operations — what takes too long, what gets missed, where the bottlenecks are. From there we identify where AI can make a real difference versus where simpler tools do the job.

Prototyping & Proof of Concept

Before committing to a full build, we prototype. You see working examples early, test them against real scenarios, and we iterate together until the approach is proven.

Integration & Deployment

Once validated, we integrate the solution into your existing workflows and tools. No rip-and-replace — we build around what you already use so adoption is seamless.

What This Looks Like

  • Automated document processing and data extraction
  • Custom chatbots for customer support or internal Q&A
  • Predictive models for forecasting and planning
  • AI-assisted content generation and review
  • Intelligent email triage and routing
02

Data Analytics & Business Intelligence

Your business generates data every day. We turn it into something useful — dashboards, reports, and models built around the questions you actually need answered.

Data Audit & Cleanup

Before building anything, we assess what data you have, where it lives, and what shape it's in. Clean data is the foundation — we help you get there without disrupting your day-to-day.

Dashboard & Report Design

Custom dashboards tailored to how your team actually works. Not a wall of charts — clear, focused views that answer specific questions and drive decisions.

Automated Reporting

Reports that build themselves. We set up automated pipelines so your weekly, monthly, and quarterly numbers are ready when you are — no manual spreadsheet work.

What This Looks Like

  • Sales and revenue dashboards with live data
  • Customer analytics and segmentation
  • Financial reporting and KPI tracking
  • Inventory and operations visibility
  • Automated weekly and monthly report delivery
03

Workflow Automation

Manual processes eat up your day. We map your workflows, connect your tools, and build automations so your team spends less time on busywork and more on what matters.

Process Mapping

We walk through your current workflows step by step — who does what, in which tools, and how long it takes. This reveals the friction points and the quick wins.

Tool Integration

Your tools should talk to each other. We connect your existing software — CRM, email, invoicing, project management — so data flows automatically instead of being copied by hand.

Custom Automation

From simple triggers to complex multi-step sequences, we build automations that handle the repetitive work. Each one is tested with your real data before going live.

What This Looks Like

  • Automated invoicing and payment reminders
  • Email sequences and follow-up workflows
  • Data entry elimination between systems
  • Client onboarding automation
  • File organization and document routing
04

Project Management Consulting

Complex projects need someone who's been in the trenches. We embed with your team to plan, organize, and drive initiatives across the finish line — on scope, on time.

Planning & Scoping

We define what done looks like before work begins. Clear scope, realistic timelines, identified risks, and a plan your team can actually follow.

Coordination & Execution

We keep things moving — managing dependencies, clearing blockers, running standups, and making sure the right people are focused on the right things at the right time.

Reporting & Handoff

Clear status updates for stakeholders without the jargon. When the project wraps, we hand off documentation and processes so your team can maintain momentum independently.

What This Looks Like

  • Technology rollouts and system migrations
  • Office relocations and setup
  • Product launches and go-to-market coordination
  • Vendor selection and onboarding
  • Cross-team initiatives with multiple stakeholders
